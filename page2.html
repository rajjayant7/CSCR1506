HTML Lists: Ordered and Unordered

Understand the differences between ordered and unordered lists in HTML.

Ordered Lists Unordered Lists
Ordered lists display items in a specific sequence:


<ol>
<li>First item</li>
<li>Second item</li>
<li>Third item</li>
</ol>

Preview:
  1. First item
  2. Second item
  3. Third item
 Unordered lists display items with bullet points:


<ul>
<li>Apple</li>
<li>Banana</li>
<li>Cherry</li>
</ul>

Preview:
  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Cherry

Summary

Ordered lists are numbered.Unordered lists use bullet points.
